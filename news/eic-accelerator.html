QuantaMap receives EIC Accelerator

Big news from QuantaMap: we have been awarded the prestigious EIC Accelerator Grant of €2.5 million, combined with an equity investment component by the EIB. This recognition places us among the 61 leading European startups selected in this round.

This will accelerate the commercialisation of our SQUID-AFM tool for quantum R&D and the development as an in-line quality control platform for quantum chip manufacturing. Our technology provides unique insights that will help the quantum industry to transition from lab to fab, with better chip performance, production consistency and yield.

The EIC funding also provides us with bandwidth to serve some more launching customers. If you want to learn more, get in touch or speak to us at our booths at the upcoming conferences:

🗓 APS March Meeting, Denver, March 16–19 2026
