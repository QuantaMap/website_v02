MKB Hightech project QUACIMI

QuantaMap and TNO kick off a project to improve the quality of spin qubits.

In our collaborative MKB Hightech project from Holland High Tech we will develop the tools necessary to improve current designs of spin qubit chips.

Within the QUACIMI project (QUantitative, Cryogenic Imaging of MIcromagnets on Quantum Chips) we will focus on developing an advanced quantitative imaging technique and the hardware and software required for it. The goal is to characterise the magnetic fields of on-chip micromagnets, which are crucial for this type of scalable quantum computer based on spin qubits.
